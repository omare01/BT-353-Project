About Duck Details

DuckDetails is an information hub which connects students to resources pertaining to the majors at Stevens Institute of Technology. DuckDetails is not affiliated with Stevens Institute of Technology.

This website was created by 5 Stevens students studying project management. We identified a need for students to easily access extra learning materials relevant to their major. The purpose of this site is to connect Stevens students to relevant study and informational materials through a nicely organized page based on their major. Our group used AtlassianTrello to follow Agile project management with Scrum to track our progress on all the different portions of the website. This project was completed over the time period of one semester, while meeting several times a week to plan our sprints, reflect on our work, and follow the “daily standups” style for quick and efficient meetings. DuckDetails is not affiliated with Stevens Institute of Technology.
