This page serves as a hub, connecting prospective and current Science students at Stevens Institute of Technology to university and outside resources s​pecific to their major.

The Department of Chemistry and Chemical Biology at Stevens has a long history of innovation in education and research with excellence in laboratory instruction. The curriculum is designed to provide interdisciplinary training, rigorous theoretical instruction and exposure to experimental methods and state-of-the-art equipment. Both the Bachelor of Science in Chemistry and the Bachelor of Science in Chemical Biology are certified by the American Chemical Society.
